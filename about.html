HI, I'M ANASTASIIA SAMOKHINA!

I am an event and wedding planner with many years of experience. My journey began in Moscow, where I first discovered the beauty of creating meaningful moments. More than 10 years ago, life brought me to Italy — a country that quickly became home to my heart and the place where my creative vision truly came to life.

Although I started my career as an architect, I soon realized that my deepest passion was not just designing spaces, but designing emotions — crafting experiences that people would carry with them forever. This is what led me to the world of event and wedding planning.

Together with my trusted team in Moscow, I chose to expand our projects to Italy, offering our services to international clients who dream of celebrating their most important moments in this extraordinary countries.

What matters most to us is creating something uniquely yours. We listen, we imagine, we design — and then we transform every detail into reality.

Our mission is to turn your story into an unforgettable experience, curated with elegance, creativity, and a deeply personal touch.
