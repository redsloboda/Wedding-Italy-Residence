SERVICES

01.
Location
Choosing the right location is the first step in turning your vision into reality. Together with you, and guided by your wishes and expectations, we identify the perfect venue — the one you've always envisioned. A space that reflects your identity and enhances every detail of your event, creating the ideal atmosphere for an unforgettable experience.
02.
Planing & Design
From weddings and milestone celebrations to corporate gatherings or film productions, design is the element that sets the stage, defining the mood and the influence of every project. It is the craft of transforming an idea into reality, aligning environment, emotion, and intention to deliver a memorable experience.
03.
Production
A result shaped through a thoughtful production process, where each stage is designed to ensure lasting impact. Every step reflects the precision, refinement, and innovation that define our approach, forming a seamless workflow that brings each concept to life with clarity and purpose.
04.
Stationary
We offer a bespoke stationery experience crafted entirely by hand by our dedicated creative team. From personalized invitations to refined event details, each piece is designed with artistry and precision. For a truly unique touch, we also provide live painting during the event, allowing guests to witness the creation of custom artworks in real time — transforming every moment into a memorable keepsake.
05.
Suppliers
We work exclusively with carefully selected and trusted vendors, ensuring that from the very first meeting you feel confident and at ease — even when planning from a distance. Our network of professionals guarantees quality, reliability, and impeccable service throughout every stage of the process.
06.
Music
We assist you in selecting and securing musicians and multilingual hosts, tailored to the tone and style of your event. Whether you envision a renowned singer or an Italian band that brings the true Dolce vita atmosphere to life, we find the perfect performers to elevate your celebration.
07.
Gifts
We take care of suggesting the most creative and practical wedding favors, ensuring that each guest leaves with a little piece of your special day. Our thoughtful selections are designed to delight and create lasting memories, making every gift a meaningful reflection of your celebration.
08.
Photos
We help you choose the photographer that best suits your style, ensuring your photographs become lifelong memories. Above all, we make sure you receive all your photos on time, capturing every moment of your special day with care and precision.
